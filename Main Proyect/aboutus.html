Perez Cruz is recognized as the most admired wine brand in South America since 2016.

History

Since 60 years ago, Pablo Pérez and Mariana Cruz discovered the perfect place to create what would soon be the Pérez Cruz Viña, on the southern bank of the Maipo Valley at the foot of the Andes Mountains. Where they found a privileged space in the Chilean countryside, full of tradition and geographical value, since the slopes located at high altitudes where they perched had thousands of years of rock formations and earthworks under their feet that made it unique, a stony and clayey soil that is complex to cultivate, rich in minerals and privileged in its Mediterranean climate.

Time Line

1963

Pablo Pérez and Mariana Cruz acquire Fundo Liguai in the heart of the Maipo Valley. Terroir recognized for its fine red wines. Meeting point and family reunion

1993

Mariana Cruz and her 11 children bring the family dream to life. The planting of the first hectares of vineyards begins. 21 hectares of Cabernet Sauvignon.

1998

Planting of an additional 120 hectares is completed.

2000

José Cruz Ovalle is in charge of the design and construction of the winery.

2002

With the name Pérez Cruz the family presents the fruit of their vision to the world.
The Reserva Cabernet Sauvignon Limited Edition, Carmenère, Syrah and Cot wine lines were created.
First Premium Liguai wine.

2009 - 2010

Due to the quality and typicity of the wines, and the reception from critics and consumers, an additional 120 hectares of red vines are planted.

2019

THE FIRST CHAPTERS OF A STORY OF PEOPLE AND PLACE. 580 total hectares 240 hectares planted. Wines grown and bottled at origin in the Maipo Valley. New wines with an accent on freshness: Grenache Limited Edition and Rosé Lingal, Chilled Wines line.
